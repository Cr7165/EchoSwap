
Phase 1: Immediate Setup (Do This Today)
1.  Assess your current situation
	•  List all wallets you currently use and how much is in each (in USD value).
	•  Identify your “main” hot wallet (Phantom/Backpack) and any cold wallets.
2.  Create a dedicated Trading Wallet
	•  Set up a new Phantom or Backpack wallet solely for trading/alert execution.
	•  Move only 1–5% of your total portfolio into this wallet.
3.  Set up a Cold/Hardware Wallet for long-term holdings
	•  Buy or use a Ledger Nano S Plus / Nano X or Trezor.
	•  Transfer 80–95% of your assets (especially SOL and stables) to this cold wallet.
	•  Never enter the seed phrase on any computer or phone.
Phase 2: Wallet Diversification Strategy
4.  Implement Multi-Wallet Structure
	•  Wallet 1: Daily/Trading (hot) – small amount for quick dip buys
	•  Wallet 2: Main HODL (cold/hardware) – bulk of your portfolio
	•  Wallet 3: Backup/Emergency – separate cold wallet stored in a different physical location
	•  Wallet 4 (optional): Staking/Yield wallet – if you stake SOL
5.  Diversify Across Chains
	•  Do not keep everything on Solana.
	•  Spread holdings: SOL + ETH + BTC + USDC/USDT (on Solana and Ethereum).
6.  Upgrade to Multi-Sig for larger amounts
	•  For any wallet holding $10k+, set up a 2-of-3 or 3-of-5 multi-signature wallet (use Squads or Gnosis Safe on Solana).
	•  Requires multiple approvals before moving funds.
Phase 3: Security Hardening
7.  Protect Your Seed Phrases
	•  Never store seed phrases digitally (no photos, no cloud notes, no notes app).
	•  Use a metal backup plate (Billfodl, Cryptosteel, or similar).
	•  Store the plate in at least 2 separate secure physical locations (safe + family member’s house, etc.).
8.  Enable Multi-Factor Authentication Everywhere
	•  Turn on 2FA (use Authenticator app, not SMS) on:
		•  Email
		•  All exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, etc.)
		•  Phantom/Backpack wallet
		•  Discord, Telegram, X accounts
9.  Improve Transaction Safety
	•  Always double-check the recipient address before confirming.
	•  Use your hardware wallet to sign important transactions when possible.
	•  Enable “blind signing protection” in Phantom if available.
10.  Revoke Permissions Regularly
	•  Go to Phantom → Connected Apps → Revoke any unused dApps or sites.
Phase 4: Ongoing Habits (Do Weekly/Monthly)
11.  Weekly Wallet Health Check
	•  Review balances across all wallets.
	•  Check for any suspicious transactions.
	•  Update your diversification if one wallet grows too large.
12.  Monthly Security Audit
	•  Change passwords on important accounts.
	•  Verify 2FA is working on everything.
	•  Confirm seed phrase backups are still secure and legible.
13.  Stay Updated
	•  Follow official wallet Twitter accounts (Phantom, Ledger, Trezor).
	•  Enable security notifications in your wallets.
